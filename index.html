FAQ

Which AirPods are supported? +

Any AirPods with the H1 or H2 chip: AirPods Pro, AirPods Pro 2, AirPods 3, and AirPods 4.

Does it drain my battery? +

No. Sampling is adaptive and the model runs on-device. In our internal tests, AirPods battery impact is under 5% over a full workday.

Is my data private? +

Yes. All motion processing happens on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

How long is the TestFlight beta? +

We're targeting a public App Store launch in the coming months. Beta testers keep free access during the program.
