Which AirPods are supported? +

Any headphones with Apple's H1 or H2 chip: AirPods 3rd generation or newer, AirPods Pro 1 or newer, AirPods 4, AirPods Max, and Beats Fit Pro.

Does it drain my battery? +

We belive the battery impact is very limited, but feel free to provide feedback on it, we're looking forward to improve.

How long is the TestFlight beta? +

We're targeting a public App Store launch in the coming months. Beta testers keep free lifetime access!
